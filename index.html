Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05th July 2025

1. Information Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All profile data extracted from LinkedIn is processed and stored locally in your browser only.

2. Data Usage

Extracted profile information (such as name, job title, email, phone, and profile URL) is used solely for the purpose of enabling you to export LinkedIn profiles to a CSV file. The data is never shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All extracted data is stored temporarily in your browser’s local storage. You can manage or delete this data at any time using the extension’s interface. No data leaves your device.

4. Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function (such as access to LinkedIn profile pages and local storage). These permissions are used exclusively for extracting and managing profile data as described.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can clear all extracted profiles or selectively remove them at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be posted here and the effective date will be updated.
